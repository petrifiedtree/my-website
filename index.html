Purpose of the Map

The purpose of the map is to clarify the image of the world before the Great Flood, as referenced in various mythologies. The map shows where the giant trees once stood and hopefully helps us see beyond Atlantis.

What might have happened before the Great Flood?

Before the natural catastrophe, almost all of humanity had shifted from biotechnology (nature, spirit, body, etc.) to a technology that requires destruction to function—at least in cities. With that technology, something was created that affected the whole world. The Vedrus, however, have always continued using biotechnology and understood its significance. In its thoughtlessness, humanity closed itself off and lost connection with both itself and the light. With light no longer able to support humanity, the world was heading toward great destruction. The events preceding the natural catastrophe are symbolically depicted in the following passages from the Book of Enoch:

  • 1 Enoch 6:1-6
  • 1 Enoch 7:1-6
  • 1 Enoch 52:1-3
  • 1 Enoch 54:1-3
  • 1 Enoch 54:4-5
  • 1 Enoch 60:1-2
